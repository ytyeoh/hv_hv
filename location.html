Hijauan Hills is the 1st fully integrated township in Seberang Perai Selatan planned for modern Malaysia city living. Named for and inspired by its surrounding landscapes.

Hijauan Hills boasts 450 acre of rolling hills, lush meadows and water-bodies, rising to a maximum height of 95 meters above sea level. It is the new Flagship project of Asas Dunia Berhad. Setting a new density benchmark of less than 10 units per acre of land.
